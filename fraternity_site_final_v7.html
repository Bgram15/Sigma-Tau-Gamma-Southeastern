About Us

Founded on tradition and driven by excellence, ΣΤΓ fosters leadership, scholarship, and lifelong brotherhood.

Sigma Tau Gamma at Southeastern Louisiana University is a brotherhood dedicated to fostering personal growth, academic achievement, and community service. Founded on the principles of virtue, diligence, and brotherly love, our fraternity provides a supportive environment where members can develop leadership skills, engage in meaningful activities, and form lifelong friendships. Through various philanthropic efforts and social events, we strive to create a positive impact both on campus and in the surrounding community. Join us as we embody the values of Sigma Tau Gamma and work together to make a difference.
